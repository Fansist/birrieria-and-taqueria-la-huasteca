Our Story

Pacific Beach's first birrieria

Birrieria & Taqueria La Huasteca opened in December 2021 as Pacific Beach's first dedicated birrieria, founded with family recipes from San Luis Potosi, Mexico. Every day, birria de borrego (lamb) and birria de res (beef) are slow-cooked until fall-apart tender, and tortillas are pressed by hand on-site.

Beyond the birria that built the name, the kitchen turns out quesabirria tacos, mulitas, birria ramen, birria pizza, and birria burritos, plus a breakfast menu of chilaquiles and menudo, and a chicken mole rooted in San Luis Potosi tradition.

“Family recipes, handmade tortillas, and San Luis Potosi heritage, brought to the beach.”

Frequently asked questions

What is birria, and which styles do you serve?

Birria is a slow-cooked Mexican stew traditionally made with goat, though beef and lamb versions are common today. We serve both birria de res (beef) and birria de borrego (lamb), prepared using family recipes from San Luis Potosi, Mexico.

Do you offer dine-in, takeout, and delivery?

Yes. We have outdoor seating for dine-in, plus takeout, and delivery through DoorDash, Grubhub, and UberEats.

What are your hours?

Hours can vary, so please call ahead at (858) 203-3902 or check our current listings before you visit. A breakfast menu with chilaquiles and menudo is available, ask about morning hours when you call.

Is the menu handmade and made to order?

Our tortillas are pressed by hand on-site every day, and our birria is slow-cooked using family recipes carried from San Luis Potosi, Mexico.

Where are you located?

We're at 865 Turquoise St in North Pacific Beach, San Diego, CA 92109, see the map and directions below.
